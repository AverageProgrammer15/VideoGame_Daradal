My Favorite Video Game!

I'm sure most of you heard of Legend of Zelda, a series made by Nintendo that started from 1986 until today! And the latest popular game release from the series is Tears of the kingdom!

Why do I love this game?

1.) Combat

The combat here is amazing. In this game, you have to fight each opponent differently. And there are different ways to counter the enemy of attacks!


One method of countering enemies are parrying! By using it at the right time you can stun the enemy for a few seconds leaving them vulnerable!


Another way of countering enemies is by dodging at the right time. This is very hard to do but will deal a lot of damage to the enemy. Some even getting stunned for a while!



2.) Exploration

The land of Hyrule is massive! Some areas requiring certain armor/clothing to survive! There are 3 types of area in the game: Sky, Overworld, and the Depths


The Sky Islands could be accessed by using vehicles that either made by the game or made by you. Some islands could hold secret treasures that could help you on your journey!


The Overworld is where most of your journey will take place. This is where most quests will take place. This area is the most lively part of the map. But this won't be the same with the last area I'm going to talk about...


This is the chasm, the most deadliest part of the game. All of the enemies here will be way more powerful than the enemies in the other parts of the map. These can be accessed by falling down from the chasms scattered across the Overworld.

3.) Story

As you and Zelda, the princess of Hyrule, explore the secrets of Hyrule, they come across Ganondorf, who is a threat against the land of Hyrule. Using his power, he seperated you and Zelda, who was teleported in a mysterious area. Your mision is to find Princess Zelda and defeat Ganondorf.

Unlike other normal Action/Adventure games, you're not alone in your journey! The sages will help you in every step of your journey and help defeat Ganondorf!



Is it worth playing?

This game is a MUST PLAY!


Everything about it (scenery,combat,exploration,story) is truly amazing! I really reccommend buying this! This game is a solid...
